Spyfall

Learn more about spyfall at boardgamegeek.com. Spyfall is a social deduction card game where players try to figure out who among them is the spy. Spyfall can be a good game for a party, as it is designed to be played with a large group of people and has a social, interactive element that encourages players to engage with each other. The game's fast-paced, unpredictable nature and the pressure to bluff and think on your feet can make for an exciting and entertaining experience.

Play spyfall at avanderw.co.za. There are many online versions of Spyfall. However, I wanted to build my own version. I started and dropped the project a few times, due to the quality of the online versions being so good. However, most of them have grown in complexity and incorporate ads nowdays. I wanted to build a simple version that is easy to use and does not require an account. After finishing the joke generator, I decided to pick up the project again. Seeing the opportunity for quick delivery.

The goal of this domain is to be accessible and very close to vanilla HTML. So the site is statically hosted on GitHub and uses their cert for HTTPS. I tried to find free hosting for a service after Heroku changed its usage policy. AWS's free offering could work, though it locked me into their ecosystem. EC2 only has 720 hours free per month. So I self-hosted, which required the cert. As with most projects, there are lessons to be had. The complete build took about half a day. GitHub copilot helped speed things up. Though my lessons came with the distribution. The first lesson is that you require a secure web socket connection (wss://) when running on HTTPS. The second lesson being you need HTTPS to access navigator.share or navigator.clipboard, and you also require HTTPS to make the site a PWA, which led me to obtain a cert for the domain I use to host the go-lang service.
