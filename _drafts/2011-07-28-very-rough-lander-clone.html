So I have been playing with Flixel a bit more and really am amazed at how fast it is to put something together. This is what I have put together in the span of the afternoon.

[swfobj src="http://avanderw.co.za/wp-content/uploads/2011/07/FlixelLander.swf" width="640" height="480"]

It contains

  • random map generation
  • simple lander physics
  • collision detection with the terrain
  • screen wrapping to the left and right
  • fuel bar indicating fuel remaining

Controls

Arrow keys to thrust and turn
~ key to see the debug console (screenshot)
